WHAT I DO

Manufacturing & Mechanical Engineering

Computer aided design (CAD), finite element analysis (FEA), computer aided manufacturing (CAM), CNC programming, process design, new product introduction, and multi-axis CNC machining. Experience with common aerospace manufacturing processes for both metallics and composites. Applied design for manufacture and assembly (DFM, DFA, DFx). Utilized FDM, SLA, and SLS 3D printing processes for rapid prototyping.

Software Development

Object-oriented programming in Java, C#, C++, and C. Web and mobile app development using JavaScript, React, React Native, HTML, and CSS. Distributed/cloud computing, services, virtualization, and storage using AWS. Relational and NoSQL database experience using a variety of services and clients. Machine learning, neural networks, and artificial intelligence using Python, R, Jupyter, and ML packages such as AWS Rekognition. Experience with Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and other IOT devices.

Play Outdoors

Snowboarding, hiking, mountain biking, and enjoying the beauty of the earth.

Some of my favorite places include Stevens Pass, Mt. Baker Ski Area, North Cascades National Park, Baker Lake, Diablo Lake, Ross Lake, and many others throughout the Pacific Northwest.
