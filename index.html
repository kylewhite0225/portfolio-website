Capstone Project
Forest ecosystems are critical to the health of the planet and therefore our own. They play an important role in climate, water retention, temperature regulation, and ecosystem services such as clean water, air, and the protection of biodiversity. Unfortunately, forests throughout the world are being destroyed and damaged on a daily basis due to human developments such as urban sprawl, fragmentation, and forest product extraction.

The mission of NCRP is to produce a platform that utilizes modern technology such as deep learning neural networks and data science to assist non-profit organizations, individuals, and communities with the identification of potential locations for successful reforestation efforts in Washington State, reducing time and cost.

We've done so by creating a mapping service that provides reforestation opportunity as well as information on common tree species within targeted areas that the user can select and search for.

Technologies include:

  • GeoServer
